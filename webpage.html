Overview
About the Conference
The International Conference on Advanced Materials for Converging Technologies (IC-AMCT-2026) aims to bring together researchers, academicians, scientists, industry professionals, and students to share their research findings and innovations in the field of advanced materials and converging technologies. The conference will provide a platform for interdisciplinary discussions and collaborations in emerging areas of materials science, chemistry, physics, and technology. Organized by the Department of Applied Physics & Chemistry (SoANS), Joy University, this hybrid conference offers both in-person and virtual participation.

Call for Abstracts / Papers

Kindly send your abstracts and research papers through email at conferencephyche@gmail.com. Abstracts are invited for Oral / Poster Presentations.

Abstracts must be typed in MS Word using Times New Roman, Font Size 12. Authors should clearly mention their names, affiliations and the corresponding author indicated by an asterisk (*). Abstracts should not exceed 250 words.

Papers will be published as Conference Proceedings or an Edited Book with ISBN.
